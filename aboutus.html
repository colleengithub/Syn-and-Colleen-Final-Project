Heyo I'm Syndra and I'm a cofunder of of Music Mania. Along with three other talented girls, we created this website as the result of a group project from our time at the Flatiron School. The Meteor Scholarship Program for 2016 has provided us with the opportunity to further our web development skills and I'm really glad to have taken a chance that's resulted in me discovering a new passion.

Colleen

Hi my name is Colleen and I am a cofounder of Music Mania. Together, Syndra, Sidra, Maylee, and I used what we learned during our time here at the Flatiron School to produce this website.
